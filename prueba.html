Development Aid - UN Reacts to Proposed Cuts
11 Nov 2015
“Helping people in need should not be a zero-sum game,” Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon said, warning against planned cuts in development aid in order to finance the response to the refugee crisis.
Source: UN News Centre
Climate Change - Greenhouse Gas Levels Reach Record High
9 Nov 2015
Greenhouse gas concentrations hit yet another record high in a continuum that is fuelling climate change and will make the planet more dangerous and inhospitable for future generations, the UN said.
Source: UN News Centre
Human Rights - Children Without a Country
3 Nov 2015
Children without a country are haunted by "discrimination, frustration and despair." The UN called for urgent action to address the problem of child statelessness the world.
Source: UN News Centre

Refugee Crisis - resettlement of Syrians Fleeing Fighting
10 Nov 2015
Countries around the world should follow Canada's example and take more refugees fleeing war-torn Syria, says UN High Commissioner for Refugees, António Guterres.
Source: UN Radio
Civil Aviation - Making Air Travel Safer
11 Nov 2015
A landmark agreement on a radio spectrum for tracking global air traffic is designed to prevent a repeat of the “tragic loss” of Malaysian Airlines Flight MH370 in March 2014.
Source: UN News Centre
UN Art & Gifts Series - Chagall Window
Celebrating peace through light and colour -- Check out Marc Chagall's masterpiece at the United Nations.
Source: UN News Centre
Health - Sierra Leone Ebola-Free
7 Nov 2015
The World Health Organization (WHO) declared that Ebola virus transmission had stopped in Sierra Leone.
Source: UN News Centre
Human Security - Egypt After the Arab Spring
3 Nov 2015
A UN Trust Fund is helping local leaders raise the self-sufficiency and business potential of rural communities in Egypt.
Source: UNTV: UN in Action
